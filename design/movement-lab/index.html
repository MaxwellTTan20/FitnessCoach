Lift & Flow

A biomechanical coaching interface that feels measured, precise, and human. No generic neon gym dashboard, no gradient dark mode.

01

Movement Lab

The app should feel like a real-time form instrument: body motion, joint angles, rep quality, and coach cues are the visual language.

02

Design Rules

  • Light instrument surfaces instead of dark glass panels.
  • Calibration ticks, angle arcs, and rep timelines as motifs.
  • Color is semantic: teal tracks, green confirms, clay corrects.
  • Coach copy stays short enough to read while moving.

What This Means For The Flutter Build

The camera screen should become the centerpiece, with analyzer data drawn as an instrument overlay rather than floating decorative cards.

Existing app flows stay intact: Auth, Home, Workouts, Record, Stats, Profile, and Session Summary. The redesign changes presentation, not product scope.

The system should support both beginner and pro modes through density: beginner sees fewer labels, pro sees more diagnostic ticks.
